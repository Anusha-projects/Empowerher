# Empowerher
Women and girls face barriers in jobs and entrepreneurship due to limited resources and guidance. This project provides an integrated platform offering career support, business guidance, and health resources to empower them with skills, confidence, and opportunities.


# EmpowerHer

An AI-powered women empowerment platform that provides:

- Career Guidance
- Resume Builder
- Health Support
- AI Chatbot
- Learning Resources
- Business Support

- HTML
- CSS

- Resume Auto Generator

- Business Booster
